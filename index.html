Silver Warrior

(Relaxed, Fair Play War Clan)

The Philosphy

Not every clan is the same. Neither are we.

Silver Warrior is a group of relaxed,
fair play, skilled CoC players.

Clan war is one of the most unique features of Clash of Clans: where in other parts of the game you need to make up a strategy to attack within seconds, war allows you plan and prepare for a whole day. Needless to say, we consider war to be the most important part of Clash of Clans.


The key to success in war is being able to get 3 stars on bases equal to your own weight; Silver Warrior is a clan of players that can do exactly that: use solid plans to get 3 star results.


This clan is built by a group of people that, while knowing how to kick ass in the game, value a mature and relaxed environment. Our ambitions are not to be one of the top clans: our ambitions are to be a clan where laidback people can fight side to side.

The Rules

Communicate

It might seem simple, but it is important: talk, talk, talk. We are a clan that would like to help each other, but if you're not open for conversation, there is not much we can do to help.

Be kind

There are actual people with feelings at the other end of the connection. Be mindful of this, and don't talk to someone in a way you wouldn't talk to them in real life.

Be serious

Maintaining a good clan is a mutual effort from members and leaders: we do expect everyone to take their efforts seriously. Use common sense and don't opt-in to war if you cannot use both attacks.

Use our toolset

We use ClashCaller and Bindle for communication. Use these properly: especially when we have a rough war, communication and structured use of the toolset are required.
